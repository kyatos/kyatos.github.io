2024/ nov/ 23

Will Barnet's The Mirror (1981).

This space is like a void. Without platform logic organising my relationship with my language and audience, I'm suddenly unsure of how or what to write. Yet there isn't an absence of logic here (complete freedom in the mainstream sense would paralyze everyone who thinks they want it, anyway).

What arises that is any different from what I write in my journal, or the notes I type to myself? Perhaps I'm thrust back into a certain obscurity from which a new kind of audience may gather and form itself.

What do I wish to say, that would conjure the you I cannot imagine yet?
