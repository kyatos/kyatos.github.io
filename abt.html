about



“Attention is the beginning of devotion.” (Mary Oliver)



Dear visitor,

I am Kia Yee or kyatos, a slow dream machine (慢梦机) building a loving world.

In this realm, I am a Singapore-based transdisciplinary poet and performance-maker. I work primarily with text and movement, though sometimes I tend to my affinity with inanimate objects. I often speak and move within the spaces of desire, speculation, futurity and ecology, with undercurrents of love and deep time.

I live and make things to demonstrate that other worlds and ways of being are possible. To this process, I play with and trouble the bounds of categories and disciplines. I do my best to resist illusory urgency, distraction and noise, in favour of ease and (re)generative attention.

In my more human form, I lead Feelers, an artist-run label within Potato Productions. I also work variously as an editor, copyeditor, copywriter, essayist and producer.

*

Dear visitor, I shall love. I hope you will too.

Lightly,
Kia Yee
