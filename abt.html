bio



“Attention is the beginning of devotion.” (Mary Oliver)

Hi, I’m kyatos / Ang Kia Yee (she/her), a transdisciplinary artist, poet, aspiring theatre director and trainee counsellor. I believe in our innate capacity to live full, realized lives that are resilient, flexible, and connected with the cosmos at large.

I build alternative worlds by writing, staging, and organizing stories, atmospheres, spaces, relationships, and groups where our ways of being depart from the 'norm'. My work involves re-visioning, re-narrating, and re-prophesying the world through encounters with the numinous, speculative, shadowy, spiritual, transcendent, and plural. In this sense, I create variants of the world as we know it. I see this work as a type of poetry, mutation, and hacking. It is magical because it is so ordinary.

As much as possible, I use nonlinear and nonpathological frames of viewing and narrating the self, and seek to illuminate the vitality & agency of nonhuman, inanimate, and/ or nonliving things. I aim to move intuitively and strategically to expand our shared vision of possible worlds and lives; proliferate mystery and beauty; and create spaces for self-inquiry, change, and release.

A loving and luminous future lies within us, if we are able to open ourselves up to the fullness of what it entails (courage, release, responsibility, pain, grief, fear). It is here and now.

In art and writing, I like to make work that is mysterious, atmospheric, temporally-layered, and unknowing. Playing across disciplines, forms, and media, my projects are anchored in the logic of poetry — what Carl Phillips describes as patterned language and the meaningful interruption of those patterns.

My work has spanned poetry, essays, short stories, plays, performances, audio walks, workshops, and video. It also encompasses counselling work and astrology readings.

I was a resident artist for the 2024 Residency at the Intercultural Theatre Institute; a participating artist for Natasha, Singapore Biennale 2022; and a resident writer for Centre 42’s New Scripts Residency 2021.

I recently initiated a theatre collective called zone (境). From 2020-2024, I was the Co-Founder & Company Lead of Feelers, a research lab of artists and designers focused on the intersections of art and technology. From 2015-2017, I co-founded and co-led theatre collective make/space.
