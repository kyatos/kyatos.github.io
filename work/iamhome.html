I am home (2020)











In I am home, a cardboard house moves across the floor. It opens mail, folds laundry, and prepares meals. Home is a fiddly bit of architecture to navigate, getting harder to live in as it gets in the way of itself. It begins to bloat, sulk, leak… Is it still home?
