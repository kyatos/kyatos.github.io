慢 ang 梦 kia 机 yee

about



“Attention is the beginning of devotion.” (Mary Oliver)



kyatos or 慢梦机 (slow dream machine)

is Ang Kia Yee, a Singapore-based transdisciplinary poet and artist who works primarily with text, movement and performance. Their work performs and speaks within the realms of desire, futurity and ecology, with an emphasis on love and deep time.

They aim to write and make things which expand our vision of what is possible, by playing with and troubling the bounds of categories & disciplines.

In their more human form, Kia Yee leads Feelers, an artist-run label within Potato Productions. They also work variously as an editor, copyeditor, copywriter, essayist and producer.
