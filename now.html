NOW




last updated: 1 june 2024

I'm currently on a life sabbatical after major burnout. Taking time to pick up the pieces, build more loving and sustainable foundations for my health, and explore potential next steps.

A key throughline of my work has always been speculating and enacting alternative presents and futures where life is more open, sustainable, and generous for all of us. I'm sitting with that persistent interest, and trying to get more specific with it.

I'm also refocusing my energies on neglected writing projects and hunkering down to get them done. Plus picking up a dormant aspiration to direct theatre shows in gradual, measured steps. Wish me luck.
