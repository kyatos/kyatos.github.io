My writing practice is varied but centred on poetry.

My work has appeared in Quarterly Literary Review Singapore, Stand Magazine, Cha: An Asian Literary Journal, SO-FAR, and Plural Art Mag. I've also written for exhibitions, including REFUSE (Singapore Art Museum), immaterial bodies (Objectifs) and Networked Bodies (Supernormal).

In 2020, I self-published a poetry chapbook titled slow dream machine (2020).
